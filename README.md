# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 2026**

Your privacy is important to us. The Syracuse Educational Opportunity Center (EOC) Mobile App is designed to protect your personal information.

## Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information directly. All data collection is conducted through secure Microsoft Forms, which are managed and hosted by Microsoft.

## Data Access and Use

Information submitted through Microsoft Forms is accessed exclusively by authorized Syracuse EOC faculty and administrators for internal reporting, program improvement, and student services purposes only.

## Third-Party Services

This app integrates with Microsoft services (Forms, SharePoint, OneDrive) for form submissions and document access. Your use of these services is governed by Microsoft's privacy policy.

## Data Security

All data submitted through Microsoft Forms is protected by Microsoft's enterprise-grade security measures. Syracuse EOC does not maintain separate databases or storage of your personal information within this app.

## Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information. Please contact Syracuse EOC administration for any privacy-related inquiries.
